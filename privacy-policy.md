# ROAMO Privacy Policy

## 1. Overview
ROAMO is a community-based platform where users share location-based content, including photos, videos, and written posts.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

By using ROAMO, you agree to this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide
- Name, username, email address  
- Profile photo  
- Content you upload (photos, videos, text, comments)  
- Locations you add or interact with  

### b. Location Data
ROAMO is built around location-based content. When you use the app:
- We may collect precise or approximate location data  
- You may manually tag locations on content  

Content posted with a location may be visible to others.

---

### c. Automatically Collected Data
We collect standard usage data, including:
- Device type, OS, and browser  
- App usage and interactions  
- IP address  

---

### d. Third-Party Services
We use third-party providers, including:
- Google Analytics (analytics)  
- Firebase (authentication and backend services)  
- Mapbox (maps and location services)  

These providers may collect and process data under their own privacy policies.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
- Operate and improve the platform  
- Display and distribute user-generated content  
- Personalize user experience  
- Monitor and prevent abuse  
- Communicate with you  
- Support advertising and future monetization features

## 4. Public Content
ROAMO is a public platform.

Anything you post—including photos, videos, text, and location data—may be visible to other users and the public.

Do not post anything you expect to remain private.

We do not guarantee confidentiality of user-submitted content.
